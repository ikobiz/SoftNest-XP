SoftNest XP is a retro-inspired app store designed to bring back the charm of early-2000s software browsing. Built in 2025 using Windows Forms and classic HTML styling, it's made to look and feel like it belongs on a Windows XP machine.

The current catalog features a mix of nostalgic tools and experimental creations, including legacy projects like BreakManager—originally developed in 2015—and newer innovations like Bloxcraft and the Google Gemini App simulator.

Whether you're here for the aesthetics, the apps, or the memories, SoftNest XP is your cozy download nook from the future that never quite happened.
